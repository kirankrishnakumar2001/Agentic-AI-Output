# Date

## Business definition
Represents reporting dates used to analyze bookings by calendar and fiscal periods.

## Business purpose
Supports period-based reporting, trend analysis, and fiscal reporting.

## Related entities
- [Date](../entities/date.md)

## Related measures
None directly; used as an analytic dimension.

## Notes
Glossary defines additional date attributes not present in DDL: day_of_week, month_number, calendar_quarter.
